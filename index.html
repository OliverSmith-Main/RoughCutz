Our Story

Not just a
barbershop.
An experience.

Rough Cutz started as a single chair in Jozi with one mission: give every man — regardless of budget — access to a premium cut and the confidence that comes with it.

We don't rush. We don't cut corners (except on your head). Every client gets our full attention, every time. The chair is your space. The result is your crown. We just sharpen it.

01
Craft First
Every cut is a studied discipline. Our barbers train constantly, pushing technique above trends.
02
Community Rooted
We're from Joburg. This city raised us and we give back — to the streets and the people in them.
03
Zero Compromise
Premium tools. Premium products. Never cut short on quality, only on time in your chair.
04
Real Vibes
Good music, no judgment. A space where you can breathe, talk, or just zone out while we work.
